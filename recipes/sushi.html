Simple Sushi Roll

Description

Learn to make simple California rolls at home with this easy-to-follow recipe.

Perfect for sushi beginners and a great way to practice rolling technique.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 4 nori sheets
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 8 oz imitation crab meat
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sesame seeds
  • Bamboo rolling mat
  • Soy sauce for serving

Instructions

  1. Cook sushi rice and season with rice vinegar.
  2. Place nori on bamboo mat, cover with rice.
  3. Add fillings in the center.
  4. Roll tightly using the mat.
  5. Moisten the nori edge to seal.
  6. Cut roll into 6-8 pieces.
  7. Serve with soy sauce and wasabi.
