Classic Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe features layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and tender pasta sheets, all baked to perfection with a golden, bubbly cheese topping.

It's a comforting and hearty dish that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add the marinara sauce to the meat mixture. Simmer for 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom. Layer with noodles, ricotta cheese, meat sauce, béchamel sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce and topped with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  7. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
