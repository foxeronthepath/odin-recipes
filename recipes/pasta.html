Carbonara Pasta

Description

A classic Roman pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.

This creamy pasta dish comes together quickly and requires just a few quality ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 1 cup Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • 6 oz guanciale or pancetta
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil for the pasta.
  2. In a bowl, whisk eggs and grated cheeses together.
  3. Cook guanciale until crispy in a large pan.
  4. Cook pasta al dente, reserve 1 cup pasta water.
  5. Add pasta to pan with guanciale, remove from heat.
  6. Quickly stir in egg mixture to create creamy sauce.
  7. Add pasta water if needed for consistency.
  8. Serve with extra cheese and black pepper.
