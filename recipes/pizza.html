Homemade Pizza

Description

A delicious homemade pizza with a crispy crust, tangy sauce, and melty cheese.

Perfect for family dinner or entertaining friends.

Ingredients

  • 2¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2¼ tsp instant yeast
  • 1½ cups warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • Favorite toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)
  • Salt and oregano to taste

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, yeast, salt in a bowl. Add water and oil.
  2. Knead dough until smooth, let rise 1 hour.
  3. Preheat oven to 450°F with pizza stone if available.
  4. Roll dough into circle, add sauce and toppings.
  5. Bake 15-20 minutes until crust is golden.
  6. Let cool slightly before slicing.
  7. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
